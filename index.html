GreedyGame powers you to merge branding experiences within your application. This creates a harmonious blend of effective branding, a seamless user experience and effective monetization.

How to start Monetizing with GreedyGame :

Signup : Go to publisher.greedygame.com and signup for your account. Simply verify your account using the link you receive via email. If you need any help around this, we’re happy to help. Just drop us a note at support@greedygame.com.

IMPORTANT TERMS

Before we get started with the integration, let’s brush through the definitions of some important terms that we’ll see referenced regularly.

Native-Units : These are non clickable textures inside your game that are dynamically branded at runtime. The native unit is seamlessly blended into the in-game textures, so the look and feel of the textures remain the same and are simply enhanced with a branding experience.

Float-Units : This is a clickable ad-unit that hovers in at non-intrusive parts of the user’s screen. When a user clicks on this unit, it expands into a dynamic HTML window called the Engagement Window. This unit is highly adaptable and can be created to fit in contextually with the app. E.g. a cricket ball as a float unit in a cricket game or a helmet in a bike racing game.

Engagement Window : The Engagement Window is a WebView activity that displays a webpage, that opens upon clicking the float unit. Users interact with the brand through meaningful engagement experiences within this window.

Campaigns : An exhaustive list of the ads that are eligible to run on your games. This list may not contain any campaigns during setup and testing but we promise to fill it up once you’re done integrating.
